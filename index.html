About Us

The Aviral Ghat Foundation is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to the noble cause of cleaning and preserving the sacred ghats of the River Ganga in Kanpur, India. With a vision to restore the pristine beauty of these ghats and ensure the continuous flow of the holy river, the foundation has been actively working for nearly two years in each of the ghats along the Ganga in Kanpur.
